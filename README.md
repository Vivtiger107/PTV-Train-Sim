This is a train game, you are a passenger who is getting points from going to the stations listed in the mission
I would reccommend to study the Victoria train map before playing this game, you can study the map here https://edge.sitecorecloud.io/stategovernc45d-cftw-production-c9ca/media/Project/TransportWebsite/Forms/victorian-train-network-map.pdf
Using this map DURING the game is cheating, since it makes the game too easy, plus, the clues are there for a reason :)
Rules:
  . You can only be in two places in this game, in a train, or in a station, there is no 'outside' in this game
  . If you are in a vline train (the purple regional trains shown on the previously stated map), then you cannot leave at a metropilitian station if you took the train from a metroplitian station, this is because vline is only suppose to be used for going from the city to regional or regional to anywhere
  . Once you complete a mission, you get a random amount of points from 1 - 5
  . This game has rush hours with trains:
    . 12-4am: Upto one hour gaps between trains
    . 4-8am: Rush hour, upto 5 mins gaps between trains
    . 8-12am: upto 5 mins gaps between trains, but the train will skip over Melbourne Central, Flagstaff and Parliament
    . 12-4pm: Rush hour with upto 5 mins gap again
    . 4-8pm: upto 15 mins gap between trains
    . 8pm-12am: upto half an hour gaps between trains
    There is a clock in the game which will help you figure out if it is rush hour or not
  . REMEMBER TO TYPE QUIT TO LEAVE THE GAME, DO NOT JUST CLOSE THE WINDOW, THIS IS TO MAKE SURE THE GAME IS ALLOWED TO SAVE
This game may still have bugs, I have tried to fix most of them but if you do encounter any, don't hesistate to report the bug

Some services may randomly terminate at Flinders Street, especially if you try and take ans eastern service from Southern Cross

WARNING: THIS GAME WILL MOST PROBABLY WORK ONLY ON MAC, BECAUSE OF THE COMMAND I USE TO MAKE THE TRAIN ANNOUNCEMENTS, IF YOU REALLY WANT TO PLAY THIS GAME ON WINDOWS OR ANOTHER PLATFORM, SEARCH UP HOW TO USE THE COMMAND ON YOUR PLATFORM
